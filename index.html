The NY List: Museums, Zoos, and other Culture (BETA)

There's so much to see in New York that it's easy to overlook the less-famous sites. Or afford the famous ones. So I put together a list of the cultural spots I could find, and made a chart based around their operating and free hours. It was hand-compiled from about 100 homepages, so mistakes may have happened. So get mad enough to email me at dan@danwin.com for corrections/additions.

The Key
  • Colored bars indicate the operating hours. Blue are the free/suggested donation times. Museums that suggest a fee have darker blue bars, and a * by their fee.
  • Click on the headers to sort the columns. Click a name or address on the chart to see the map point.
  • The "Yelp" column draws in the number of reviews and average rating, as one measure of popularity.
  • The "Fee" column shows the standard adult ticket and membership price.
  • The map shows the free/pay status of today (doesn't yet account for holidays and such), with bigger icons for more popular places

Read more about this list, contact me at dan@danwin.com or follow me @dancow on Twitter.
