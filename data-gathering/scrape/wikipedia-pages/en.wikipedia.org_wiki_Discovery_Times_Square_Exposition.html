Discovery Times Square Exposition

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Discovery Times Square Exposition (DTSX) is an exhibition space in New York City that opened June 24, 2009 that specializes in traveling exhibitions.

It is one of three exhibitions that cater to Times Square tourists (the others are Madame Tussauds and Ripley's Believe It or Not!.)

Operated by Discovery Communications and Running Subway Productions, it is located in Times Square in the basement in the former printing plant area of the Times Square Building (the former New York Times Building).[1]

[edit] Exhibitions

  • Titanic: the artifact exhibition (2009)
  • Lucy's Legacy: the hidden treasures of Ethiopia (2009)
  • Leonardo da Vinci’s Workshop (2009)
  • King Tut (2010)
