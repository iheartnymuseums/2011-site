New York Tattoo Museum
Established 2010
Location 203 Old Town Road, Staten Island, New York 10305, USA
Director Dozer

The New York Tattoo Museum is a museum that opened in 2010. Located at 203 Old Town Road in Staten Island's Old Town neighborhood, it is reported to be the first tattoo museum to open in New York City.[1]
